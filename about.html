Five&Co is a web design studio based in Hamilton, New Zealand. We exist for one reason: small businesses deserve a great website without paying an agency fortune to get one.

Too many good Kiwi businesses are stuck with slow, outdated, cookie-cutter websites. Either the big agencies are too expensive, or the DIY tools produce something that looks exactly like everyone else's.

We exist to fix that. Custom-built sites, honest pricing, modern tech stack, real results.
