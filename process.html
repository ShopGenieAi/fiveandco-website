How We Work

Our Process

From first conversation to live website, here's exactly what working with Five&Co looks like. No surprises, no runaround.

Average project timeline: 2–4 weeks.

Start Your Project
01
Discovery
Brief & Goals
We kick off with a conversation about your business, your goals and your audience. We collect brand assets, agree on a site map, and make sure we're 100% aligned before a single line of code gets written.
02
Design
Visual Direction
A visual mockup is created showing the layout, colour palette, and design direction. You sign off before we build. No surprises and no costly back-and-forth once development begins.
03
Build
Full Site Development
Your site is built from scratch using modern technology. Content integrated, mobile optimised and technical SEO set up. We target top Google performance scores as a baseline, not a stretch goal.
04
Review
Round One Feedback
You review the full site and send through consolidated feedback. We make the amendments. Two revision rounds are included in the build fee, keeping things clean and efficient.
05
Revise
Final Polish
Round two. Final tweaks, copy updates, last-minute refinements. We button everything up and make sure you're 100% happy before going live.
06
Launch
Go Live
Your domain is connected, everything is configured and your site goes live on secure, enterprise-grade hosting. We handle every technical detail. You just need to tell people about it.
07
Handover
Client Walkthrough
We walk you through the live site, show you how Google Analytics and Search Console work. Full transparency on everything we've built.
08
Ongoing
Hosting & Support
Monthly hosting, maintenance, and support kicks in at $50/month. Additional changes billed hourly at $75–$100/hr. Your site stays fast, secure, and up-to-date.
